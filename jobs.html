Website Content Coordinator

Reference Number: SEA12

Salary: AUD 78,000 - AUD 88,000 per year
Reporting Line: Digital Communications Manager

Job Description

The Website Content Coordinator is responsible for updating and maintaining website content, ensuring information is accurate, up-to-date, and easy for users to understand.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and update website content using HTML and CMS tools such as WordPress.
  • Coordinate content publication schedules with internal teams.
  • Ensure content aligns with accessibility and brand standards.
  • Review web pages for accuracy, readability, and relevance.

Requirements

Essential:

  1. Experience updating and managing website content.
  2. Strong written communication and proofreading skills.
  3. Understanding of basic HTML and content management systems.

Preferable:

  • Experience in the sustainability or energy sector.
  • Knowledge of web accessibility principles.
  • Familiarity with SEO best practices.

Digital Project Support Officer

Reference Number: ENR47

Salary: AUD 82,000 - AUD 95,000 per year
Reporting Line: Technology Delivery Lead

Job Description

The Digital Project Support Officer assists with planning and coordinating web-based initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Support website updates and digital projects.
  2. Track deadlines and tasks.
  3. Work with teams using tools like Jira and Confluence.
  4. Prepare progress updates.

Requirements

Essential:

  • Experience in project coordination.
  • Strong organisational skills.
  • Ability to work with multiple stakeholders.

Preferable:

  • Experience with website projects.
  • Knowledge of Jira or Confluence.
  • Interest in sustainability.
